Biryani

Description

Biryani is a celebration of all that is great about Indian food! The aromas, the vibrant colour, that fluffy rice.

Ingredients

  • Rice
  • Veggies
  • Oil
  • water
  • Salt
  • Spices
  • Curd

Steps

  1. Add all the spices and vegetables to the curd
  2. Cook this in a pan and add salt
  3. Boil the rice
  4. Add the boiled rice to this cooked vegetables
